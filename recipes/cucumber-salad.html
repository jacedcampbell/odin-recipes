Cucumber Salad



Ingredients

  • 1 pound seedless cucumbers, thinly sliced crosswise on a mandoline.
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt.
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons red wine vinegar.
  • 1/2 small onion, thinly sliced.

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, toss the cucumber slices with sugar and salt and let stand for 5 mins. Stir in the vinegar and onion and refrigerate for 10 minutes, then serve.
